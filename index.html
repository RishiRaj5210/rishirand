1) We used these books as references: - Internet of Things (IoT) for Commercial Applications by John G. Shedd (2018). This book provides an overview of the commercial IoT market, including the different types of devices, applications, and challenges.

- A book written by Kevin Ashton "The Internet of Things: How the Next Evolution of the Internet is Changing Everything". This book is a seminal work on IoT, and it provides a comprehensive overview of the technology.

- The Industrial Internet of Things: Connecting Physical Systems and the Internet by Michael Chui, James Manyika, and Michael Osborne (2015). This book focuses on the Industrial IoT, discussing how it can be used to improve manufacturing, energy, and other industries.
